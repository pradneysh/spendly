Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you add — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and personalise the Spendly service for you. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filters by date or category. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app; these providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for independent purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
